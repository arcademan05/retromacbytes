Japanese Grammar and Vocabulary Practice

Welcome to the Japanese practice page. You will be asked the meaning of a Japanese word and your job is to type the English meaning into the text box. Press 'Go' or simply press 'Enter' on your keyboard to submit your answer. You will then be told wether your answer is right or wrong. You can either try again, or move onto the next question. Your missed questions and the correct translations will be displayed at the end of the quiz so you will know what to study.
Press 'Start' to begin. Good Luck!
